# Source: Integrating Digital Twins & Crop Recommendation

**Title**: Integrating digital-twins and advanced crop recommendation
**Status**: Analyzed

## Key Findings
- **System**: Cyber-Physical System (CPS) for "What-if" simulations.
- **Impact**: 10-25% yield increase; 20-30% water saving.
- **Bottleneck**: Data interoperability and high infrastructure costs for smallholders.

## Relevance to CPDT
- Highlights the need for a "Low-Cost / High-Data" balance.
- Our "District-Stratified" approach (Odisha Pilot) is the answer to the "Smallholder Infrastructure" bottleneck.

## Research Gaps Addressed by CPDT
- **Universal Scalability**: We are building a multi-district framework rather than a single-farm silo.
- **Explainability**: Bridges the "AI Black Box" with LangGraph logic.
