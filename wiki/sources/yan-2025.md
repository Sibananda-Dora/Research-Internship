# Source: Multiple Hybrid ML Models (Yan et al., 2025)

**Title**: Crop Yield Time-Series Data Prediction Based on Multiple Hybrid Machine Learning Models
**Status**: Analyzed

## Key Findings
- **Best Model**: Weighted Ensemble of **Random Forest + XGBoost**.
- **Metric Improvement**: Reduced MAE from 0.0177 (RF) to 0.0042 (Hybrid).
- **Key Feature**: Pesticide usage was identified as a critical (but high-friction) variable.

## Relevance to CPDT
- Establishes the **Track A (Tabular)** baseline architecture.
- Confirms that ensemble methods significantly outperform single tree-based models.

## Research Gaps Addressed by CPDT
- **Failure Labeling**: They predict yield only; we predict the binary "Failure Anomaly" via $Q_1$ logic.
- **Pesticide Proxy**: Since real-time pesticide data is scarce in Odisha, our `GWETROOT` and `RH2M` act as proxies for environmental stress.
