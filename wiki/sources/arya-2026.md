# Source: A Time-Series Hybrid Multi-Model ML Framework (Arya et al., 2026)

**Title**: A Time-Series Hybrid Multi-Model Machine Learning Framework for Staple Crops Yield Prediction
**Status**: Analyzed

## Key Findings
- **Top Performers**: ETS-ANN and LSTM models showed superior accuracy for Rice ($R^2$: 98.4%).
- **Crop Variability**: Low accuracy for Bananas ($R^2$: 20%) suggests high sensitivity to micro-climatic factors.
- **Hybrid Approach**: Combining traditional time-series (ETS) with DL (ANN) captures both linear and non-linear patterns.

## Relevance to CPDT
- Validates our **Track B (LSTM)** choice for sequential data.
- Highlights the risk of "Crop Variability"—we must ensure the Odisha model is specifically tuned for regional staples (Rice/Wheat).

## Research Gaps Addressed by CPDT
